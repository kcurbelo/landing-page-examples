What Makes Our Events Different?

We are the best at (1) Walking the talk and (2) making deep work fun (even funny!)

We won’t pretend racism, sexism, homophobia, trauma, addiction or mental illness aren’t real or huge challenges

We don’t make big promises we can’t keep. We treasure integrity and accountability

We won’t shame or pressure you into doing woo-woo stuff

We don’t promise that one workshop will change your life (We know you’re smarter than that!) but we can promise we’ll get you (re)started!

We don’t tell you what to do, we teach you how to figure that out for yourself now and always!

We aren’t afraid to admit we still have a lot to learn. (But that doesn’t stop us from offering the wisdom we’ve already gained! )

We are silly and whimsical! We love to laugh and inject fun into our work. Deep work doesn’t have to be hard, painful or heavy!

We won’t bash you over the head with the Law of Attraction, or tell you you created all the struggles and obstacles you face

We are sisters!

WHAT WE DON'T TEACH!

  • That positive thinking will remove all your obstacles
  • That the road to growth and success always goes upward, or is all springtime meadows full of flowers and butterflies if you simply imagine it so
  • External strategies and tactics to change your organization, community or movement
  • That success should look and feel the same way for everyone
  • That we – or some other guru or entity – has all your answers
